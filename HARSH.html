BTS - Beyond the Scene

BTS is a KPOP boy band from BigHit Entertainment. The name "BTS" stands for Bulletproof Boyscouts in Korean, but they have recently changed their English name to Beyond the Scene. They have seven members, consisting of 3 rappers and 4 vocalists. BTS made their debut on June 12th, 2013. Their dedicated fanbase is known as "Army". The group has achieved remarkable milestones, including attending the Billboard Awards in America and winning the Top Social Artist Awards.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American pop and country music singer-songwriter. Several of her songs, including "Shake It Off" (2014), "Blank Space" (2014), and "Look What You Made Me Do" (2017), topped the Billboard Hot 100 charts. She has received numerous awards for her music.
